Mode selection:
Red [0]: Absolute saturation and lightness
Red [128]: Relative saturation, absolute lightness
Red [255]: Relative saturation and lightness

Relative mode:
Green [0-127]: Desaturate
Green [128-255]: Oversaturate 
Blue [0-127]: Lighten
Blue [128-255]: Darken

Absolute mode:
Green [0-255]: Saturation
Blue [0-255]: Lightness
